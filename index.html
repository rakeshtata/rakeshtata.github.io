Services Offered

Architectural Drawings

Aesthetics occupy a critical phase in building design. Hence, we at S.S.Associates strive to develop coherent design proposals, to convince clients on the merits of drawings and designs following a set of conventions, which notably include the floor plans and elevations with adherence to quality and standards.

Structural Drawings

A resilient structure honing towards proper stability and strength caters to safety during its intended life. We at SS Associates, with a team of vastly experienced professionals, corroborate structural drawings are as per codes and specifications, ensuring safe and quality design.

Interior Designs and Execution

It is imperative to design and make interior spaces suitable to live and work in. Possessing a team of skilled people who pioneer in the usage of elements of design and organizing interior spaces in functional and aesthetic ways, SS associates deliver quality interiors based on the budget and time constraints of the client.

Permission Approvals and Paper Work

Acquiring prior permissions from the concerned local bodies and regulatory authorities before constructing a structure is cardinal and tedious. However, SS associates possess an abundance of cognizance and experience and guide the client on securing important and valid permission documents, namely structural and architectural permission drawings, Predcr, etc. We also ensure that the client obtains planning permissions as soon as possible to minimize abortive design costs.

Construction Services

So, as a leading provider of engineering and construction services like plumbing, electrical services, etc. We can assure the safe delivery of the project upon its construction.
